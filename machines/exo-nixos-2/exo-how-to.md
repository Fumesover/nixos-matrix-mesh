# Welcome to albin's matrix server.

Two web clients are available, but any other client may be used.
- https://cinny.exo.parou.eu/
- https://element.exo.parou.eu/

To register an account, ensure you use the same username as your irc username.
Reach out to albin directly to get a registration token.

Once connected, you must configure your IRC connection: Open a direct message
with `@appservice-irc:matrix.exo.parou.eu` WITHOUT ENCRYPTION:
- With cinny: click `Direct messages` on the left -> create-chat.
- With element: `Send a Direct Message` on the main page.

Send it the following messages:
- `!help`
- `!username your-irc-username`
- `!storepass your-irc-password`

Then connect to the server, and join channels:
- `!reconnect`
- `!join #team`
- `!join #platform`

Here you are all set!
